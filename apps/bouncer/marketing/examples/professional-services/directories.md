## Listing Optimization Checklist

### Profile Basics
- [ ] Accurate business name (match exactly across all listings)
- [ ] Correct address and phone (NAP consistency)
- [ ] Website URL
- [ ] Business hours
- [ ] Years in practice

### Professional Content
- [ ] Detailed service descriptions
- [ ] Practice areas/specialties
- [ ] Bar/license numbers (if applicable)
- [ ] Education and credentials
- [ ] Professional memberships
- [ ] Awards and recognition

### Photos
- [ ] Professional headshots (all attorneys/partners)
- [ ] Office exterior
- [ ] Office interior
- [ ] Team photos

### Trust Signals
- [ ] Client testimonials (where allowed)
- [ ] Case results (where allowed)
- [ ] Published articles
- [ ] Speaking engagements
- [ ] Pro bono work

## Ethical Considerations

### Legal Profession
- Check state bar advertising rules
- Some states prohibit certain claims
- "Specialist" may require certification
- No guarantee of results

### Accounting Profession
- AICPA Code of Conduct
- State board regulations
- CPA title restrictions

### General
- Never incentivize reviews
- Respond professionally to negative reviews
- Maintain confidentiality
- Accurate credential representation
