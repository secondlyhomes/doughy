## Listing Optimization Checklist

For each directory:

### Profile Basics
- [ ] Accurate business name
- [ ] Correct address and phone
- [ ] Website URL
- [ ] Business hours (including holidays)
- [ ] Price range indicator

### Content
- [ ] Compelling description (use keywords naturally)
- [ ] Cuisine type(s)
- [ ] Dining options (dine-in, takeout, delivery)
- [ ] Reservations availability
- [ ] Payment methods accepted

### Photos (Essential)
- [ ] Exterior shot (helps people find you)
- [ ] Interior ambiance (2-3 photos)
- [ ] Popular dishes (5-10 photos)
- [ ] Drinks/cocktails
- [ ] Staff/chef photos

### Menu
- [ ] Upload current menu
- [ ] Update for seasonal changes
- [ ] Include prices
- [ ] Mark dietary options (V, VG, GF)

## Review Management

### Response Time
- Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
- Prioritize negative reviews

### Positive Review Response Template
> Thank you so much for your kind words, [Name]! We're thrilled you enjoyed [dish/experience]. We can't wait to welcome you back!

### Negative Review Response Template
> We're sorry your experience didn't meet expectations, [Name]. We'd love the chance to make this right. Please reach out to [email/phone] and ask for [Manager]. We hope to see you again.

### Review Generation
- QR codes on tables linking to Google review
- Receipt footer with review request
- Post-visit email (if collecting emails)
- Personal ask from server after positive feedback
