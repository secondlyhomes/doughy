# E-Commerce Directory Listings

## Marketplaces

| Platform | Commission | Priority | Notes |
|----------|------------|----------|-------|
| Amazon | 8-45% | High | Largest marketplace, FBA available |
| eBay | 12-15% | Medium | Auctions + fixed price |
| Walmart Marketplace | 8-15% | Medium | Growing competitor to Amazon |
| Etsy | 6.5% + listing fees | High (if handmade) | Handmade, vintage, craft supplies |
| Mercari | 10% | Low | Resale marketplace |

## Comparison Shopping Engines

| Platform | Type | Priority | Notes |
|----------|------|----------|-------|
| Google Shopping | Free + Paid | Required | Requires Merchant Center setup |
| Bing Shopping | Paid | Medium | Microsoft Advertising integration |
| PriceGrabber | Free | Low | Price comparison aggregator |
| Shopzilla | Paid | Low | Shopping aggregator |

## Review & Trust Platforms

| Platform | Cost | Priority | Notes |
|----------|------|----------|-------|
| Google Business Profile | Free | Required | Even for online-only stores |
| Trustpilot | Free tier | High | Most recognized review platform |
| Better Business Bureau | Paid accreditation | Medium | Trust signal |
| ResellerRatings | Paid | Low | E-commerce specific reviews |
